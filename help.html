Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new event I've attended?
To add a new event, click the "+" button in the bottom right corner of the dashboard. Fill out the event details including name, location, date, attendance, and revenue information. You can also upload images and provide a review of your experience.
Can I edit my event reviews after posting?
Yes, you can edit your event reviews for up to 30 days after posting. Navigate to "My Events" in the menu, find the event you want to edit, and click the "Edit Review" button. After 30 days, reviews are locked but you can contact support if changes are needed.
How is the revenue information displayed?
Revenue information is displayed in ranges to protect vendor privacy while still providing useful insights. You can choose to share your specific earnings or use the default ranges. This setting can be adjusted in your profile privacy settings.
What's the difference between a vendor and event coordinator account?
Vendor accounts can add event reviews, search for events, and connect with other vendors. Event coordinator accounts can create event listings, promote events to vendors, and receive applications. Each account type has specialized features tailored to their needs.
How do I contact an event coordinator?
You can contact event coordinators through the messaging system. Navigate to the event page, and click the "Contact Coordinator" button. This will open a new message thread where you can communicate directly with the event organizer.
